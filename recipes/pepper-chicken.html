Pepper Chicken

Ingredients

For Chicken Marination

  • 1 kg Chicken leg (thighs & drumsticks) चिकन
  • 2 heaped tbsp Black peppercorns, crushed, कुटी हुई काली मिर्च
  • 5-6 sprig Curry leaves, करी पत्ते
  • Prepared Ginger Garlic paste, अदरक लहसुन का पेस्ट
  • 2 tbsp Oil, तेल

For Ginger Garlic Paste

  • 1 ½ inch Ginger (peeled & roughly chopped) अदरक
  • 6-7 no. Garlic cloves, लहसुन
  • 2-3 no. Green chillies (less spicy & roughly torn) हरी मिर्च
  • Salt to taste, नमक स्वादअनुसार

For Chicken Pepper Fry

  • ⅓ cup Oil, तेल
  • 2-3 sprig Curry leaves, करी पत्ते
  • 4 no. medium Onions, chopped, प्याज
  • Salt to taste, नमक स्वादअनुसार
  • Marinated Chicken, मैरीनेटेड चिकन
  • 1 ½ cups Curd, beaten, दही
  • 2-3 tbsp Prepared masala, तयार किया हुआ मसाला
  • 1-2 tsp Black pepper powder, काली मिर्च पाउडर

For Masala

    ½ tbsp Coriander seeds, धनिये के बीज
    ½ tbsp Cumin seeds, जीरा
    2 tbsp Black peppercorns, काली मिर्च के दाने
    6-7 no. Cloves, लौंग
    10-12 no. Black cardamom, बड़ी इलायची
    Salt to taste, नमक स्वादअनुसार

For Garnish

  • Coriander sprig, धनिया पत्ता
  • Lemon wedge, नींबू की फांक

Process

  1. In a bowl, add chicken, black peppercorns, curry leaves, prepared ginger garlic paste, oil and marinate well. Keep it aside for further use.
  2. In a mortar pestle, add ginger, garlic, green chillies, salt to taste and grind it into the coarse paste. Keep it aside for further use.
  3. In a handi, add oil, once it's hot, add curry leaves, onions, salt to taste and cook well until it's translucent.
  4. Add marinated chicken and cook it for 7-8 minutes on medium flames.
  5. Add curd, prepared masala and cook it all together for a while.
  6. Once chicken gets cooked very well then add black pepper powder and mix it well.
  7. Switch off the flames and transfer it into the serving dish or bowl.
  8. Garnish it with coriander sprig and lemon wedge.
  9. Serve hot with malabar paratha and roti.
  10. In a pan, add coriander seeds, cumin seeds, black peppercorns, cloves, black cardamom, salt to taste and roast for a while or until it's aromatic
  11. Switch off the flames and transfer it into the mixer grinder jar and grind it into the smooth fine powder.
  12. Keep it aside for further use
